Intro

I'm a passionate software developer focusing on cybersecurity, AI, and cloud technologies. Right now, I'm working on my Master's in Computer Science, specializing in Cybersecurity. I love combining what I learn in class with hands-on experience. My go-to tools are C++, Python, and I'm pretty good with networking and finding security weak spots.

I get a real kick out of building secure and smart tech solutions. So far, I've created a quiz app that works with databases and a blockchain project leveraging Solidity and smart contracts that makes cryptocurrency transactions safer. These projects show off my skills and my forward-thinking approach. I'm always looking for new ways to blend software development with cybersecurity. My goal? To join a company where I can use my skills to make a real difference in the tech world. Curious about my capabilities? Take a peek at my work.

Work

My journey in tech started with a DevOps internship at Aryagami Cloud Services. There, I worked on a cool project for telecom companies. We built a platform that helps these companies make money from their services and keep track of billing for all their customers, whether they pay before or after using the service. The best part? We used microservices to make the whole system work better and grow easier.

Later, as a Junior Software Developer at Executive Softway Guild India, I got to sink my teeth into some serious data work. I created a data analytics tool using Python for MBSi Corp, a big player in auto finance tech. This wasn't just any tool – it could crunch through tons of financial data and spit out real-time insights about how loans were performing and how risky they were. To make it user-friendly, I added some snazzy interactive dashboards. And since we were dealing with sensitive info, I made sure everything was locked down tight to follow all the rules. In the end, this project gave MBSi Corp a powerful way to make smarter decisions based on solid data. It was pretty exciting to see how my work could make such a big difference in how a company operates.
